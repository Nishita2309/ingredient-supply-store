Our Hygiene and Safety Measures

Zero-Touch Delivery

All deliveries are contactless to ensure your safety.

Regular Warehouse Sanitization

Our warehouses are cleaned frequently with disinfectants.

Fever Screening

All staff undergo regular fever screening using infrared thermometers.

Hygiene Training

All employees are trained in hygiene practices and COVID-19 protocols.

Masks & Sanitizers

Delivery staff are equipped with masks and hand sanitizers.

Inventory Disinfection

All crates and items are disinfected regularly for maximum safety.
